
# Brief description of the game:
You pilot a single-seat fighter-class spacecraft, responsible for combat, navigation, and resource extraction. Your mission is to infiltrate the hostile location your ship has been teleported into, eliminate enemy threats, locate the extraction site, and secure the valuable resource hidden within. Once the objective is complete, you must find the return teleporter and safely deliver the resource back to base.

The game features a wide variety of levels designed as underground tunnel systems, including environments such as lava caves, crystal caverns, and mushroom-infested caves. Each location offers a distinct visual style, unique tunnel layouts, and environmental hazards. For example, lava caves expose the player to deadly heat and flowing magma, while mushroom caves are filled with toxic spores and poisonous gas emitted by the surrounding fungi.
